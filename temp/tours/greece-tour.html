9.8 Superb

Greece

Overview

Embark on a timeless adventure through the heart of ancient civilization with our Greek Odyssey tour. From the majestic ruins of Athens to the sun-drenched islands of the Aegean, this journey blends myth, history, and breathtaking landscapes. Explore iconic landmarks like the Acropolis and Delphi, stroll through charming coastal towns, and indulge in authentic Greek cuisine. Whether you're tracing the steps of legendary heroes or relaxing by crystal-clear waters, this unforgettable experience offers the perfect balance of culture, exploration, and leisure.

  • 7 Days
  • Greece

Tour Plan

01

Day 1: Arrival in Athens

Arrive in Athens and transfer to your hotel. After checking in, take a walk through the historic Plaka district, lined with charming streets, local shops, and traditional tavernas. In the evening, enjoy a welcome dinner with breathtaking views of the illuminated Acropolis.

02

Day 2: Athens Exploration

Spend the day exploring the iconic landmarks of Athens, starting with a guided tour of the Acropolis, Parthenon, and Ancient Agora. Visit the National Archaeological Museum, home to Greece’s most valuable artifacts. In the evening, enjoy free time to explore the city at your own pace, dine at a rooftop restaurant, or experience Athens’ vibrant nightlife.

03

Day 3: Ferry to Myokons

Take a morning ferry to Mykonos, known for its whitewashed buildings and picturesque alleys. After checking in to your hotel, relax at Paradise Beach or stroll through the charming town. As the sun sets, head to Little Venice to enjoy a beautiful view over the Aegean Sea, followed by dinner at a seaside restaurant.

04

Day 4: Mykonos Free Day

05

Day 5: Ferry to Santorini

Board a morning ferry to Santorini, the island famous for its blue-domed churches and stunning caldera views. After checking in, explore the village of Oia, where you can take in the breathtaking sunset from one of the most famous viewpoints in the world. Enjoy a relaxed dinner at a cliffside restaurant overlooking the Aegean.

06

Day 6: Santorini Highlights

Start the day with a visit to Santorini’s unique Red Beach and Black Sand Beach. Later, head to a traditional vineyard for a wine tasting experience, enjoying the island’s famous volcanic wines. In the afternoon, relax at Ammoudi Bay, where you can take a swim or enjoy fresh seafood by the water. End your trip with a final sunset view over the caldera.

07

Day 7: Departure

Enjoy a relaxed morning before transferring to the airport for your departure. Depending on your flight time, take a last stroll through Fira for some souvenir shopping or enjoy a final Greek coffee overlooking the sea.
