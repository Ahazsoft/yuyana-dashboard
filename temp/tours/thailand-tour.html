Thailand

Overview

This itinerary provides a perfect mix of adventure and relaxation, including an unforgettable visit to the elephant sanctuary, a day trip to Coral Island, an exciting safari, a scenic dinner cruise on the Chao Phraya River, and leisure time for shopping in vibrant Bangkok

  • 7 Days
  • Thailand, Bangkok

Tour Plan

01

Day 1: Arrival in Bangkok and transfer to Pattaya

Fly to Bangkok and get transferred to Pattaya and check into your hotel. Spend the day relaxing at Jomtien Beach, enjoying the sun, swimming, or engaging in water sports. In the evening, explore the lively Pattaya Walking Street, known for its vibrant nightlife, bars, and street performances. Enjoy a seaside dinner at one of the many beachfront restaurants. Highlight: Jomtien Beach, Pattaya Walking Street.

02

Day 2: Coral Island (Koh Larn)

Take a boat trip to Coral Island (Koh Larn), where you can relax on the white sandy beaches, snorkel in clear waters, or try out water sports like parasailing. After enjoying lunch on the island, spend more time soaking in the tropical atmosphere. In the evening, return to Pattaya and enjoy a leisurely dinner.

03

Day 3: Elephant Sanctuary

In the morning, head to the Pattaya Elephant Sanctuary, where you’ll learn about elephant conservation and interact with rescued elephants. You’ll have the opportunity to feed, bathe, and take a walk with these gentle giants. Afterward, return to Pattaya and spend the afternoon at leisure, perhaps visiting Pattaya Viewpoint or enjoying a relaxing spa experience. Highlight: Pattaya Elephant Sanctuary.

04

Day 4: Travel to Bangkok & Dinner Cruise

Check out from your hotel in Pattaya and travel to Bangkok (approximately 2 hours by road). After checking into your hotel, visit Wat Arun, a stunning temple by the river. In the evening, enjoy a scenic dinner cruise along the Chao Phraya River, where you’ll get a beautiful view of the illuminated temples and the city’s skyline while enjoying a delicious Thai meal. Highlight: Wat Arun, Chao Phraya River Dinner Cruise.

05

Day 5: Safari World

Spend the day at Safari World Bangkok, an exciting open-air zoo and marine park. Take a safari drive to see various wild animals like giraffes, zebras, tigers, and lions in a natural habitat. Enjoy animal shows, such as the dolphin and bird performances, and explore the safari park. Return to your hotel in the evening for some rest

  • Safari World Bangkok
  • Animal shows
  • Safari drive
06

Day 6: Shopping in Bangkok

Dedicate the day to shopping at Siam Square, Siam Paragon, and MBK Center, where you can find fashion, electronics, and souvenirs. Afterward, explore Chinatown for local markets and street food. In the evening, unwind with a Thai massage or visit Asiatique Riverfront for more shopping and dining options.

07

Day 7: Departure from Bangkok

Spend your final morning in Bangkok at leisure. You may wish to visit Chatuchak Market for last-minute shopping or relax at a luxurious spa for a traditional Thai massage. Afterward, head to the airport for your flight back to Addis Ababa.
