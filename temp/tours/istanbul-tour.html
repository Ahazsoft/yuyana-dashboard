Istanbul

Overview

This itinerary combines relaxing leisure time with the best of Istanbul’s scenic tours, including the Bosphorus Cruise and a peaceful Princes’ Island Tour.

  • 7 Days
  • Istanbul

Tour Plan

01

Day 1: Arrival in Istanbul

Arrive in Istanbul and transfer to your hotel. Spend the day relaxing and adjusting to the local time zone. Explore the Sultanahmet area for a casual stroll, take in the city’s charm, and enjoy a meal at a local restaurant.

02

Day 2: Bosphorus Cruise

Take a scenic Bosphorus Cruise today. Enjoy the stunning views of Istanbul from the water, passing by iconic landmarks like Dolmabahce Palace, Rumeli Fortress, and the Bosphorus Bridge. After the cruise, enjoy a visit to the Spice Bazaar, where you can shop for spices, sweets, and local goods.

03

Day 3: Princes’ Island Tour

Embark on a full-day trip to the Princes’ Islands, an escape from the city’s hustle. Visit Büyükada and Heybeliada, the car-free islands perfect for cycling or walking. Explore Aya Yorgi Church for panoramic views, relax on the beach, and enjoy the tranquil atmosphere.

04

Day 4: Leisure Day

This day is for you to relax and enjoy Istanbul at your own pace. You can explore the vibrant Istiklal Street, visit cafes, or relax in the parks. You may also choose to visit the Galata Tower for stunning views of the city or enjoy a Turkish bath experience.

05

Day 5: Taksim and Beyoglu District

Spend the day exploring the lively Beyoglu District and Taksim Square, two of Istanbul’s most dynamic areas. Walk along Istiklal Street, which is full of shops, cafes, and galleries. Stop by the Pera Museum or take a relaxing stroll in Gezi Park. You can also check out Cihangir, a bohemian neighborhood known for its cafes and street art.

06

Day 6: Relax and Enjoy Istanbul’s Cafes

Take another relaxed day and visit some of Istanbul’s famous cafes in areas like Karaköy or Nişantaşı. Enjoy leisurely meals, shop at high-end boutiques, or just relax in the city’s vibrant atmosphere. You can also visit the Dolmabahce Palace or enjoy a sunset walk by the Bosphorus.

07

Day 7: Departure

On your final day, enjoy some last-minute shopping or take a peaceful walk along the Bosphorus before transferring to the airport for your departure.
