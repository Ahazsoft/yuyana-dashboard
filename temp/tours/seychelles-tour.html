Seychelles Getaway

Overview

  • 5 Days
  • Seychelles

Tour Plan

01

Day 1: Arrival in Seychelles

Arrive in Seychelles and transfer to your hotel. Spend the rest of the day at your leisure, resting and acclimating to the tropical environment. You can relax by the pool, enjoy the hotel’s amenities, or take a stroll along the beach.

02

Day 2: Relaxed Beach Day

Spend the day enjoying the beach at your own pace. You can swim in the crystal-clear waters, lounge on the white sandy beaches, or visit nearby cafes. It’s your day to relax and take in the stunning surroundings.

03

Day 3: Snorkeling Adventure

Head out for a snorkeling excursion to explore the vibrant coral reefs and marine life of Seychelles. The trip includes a delicious lunch and an opportunity to swim with tropical fish in the clear, warm waters

04

Day 4: Leisure Day

Enjoy a day at your own leisure. Whether you choose to visit local markets, enjoy more time on the beach, or explore the nearby nature trails, the day is yours to enjoy at your own pace.

05

Day 5: Departure

After a relaxing few days, it’s time to head to the airport for your flight back, taking with you beautiful memories of your tropical getaway.
