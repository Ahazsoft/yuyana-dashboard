City Tour

EXPLORE THE LEGENDARY CITY OF ADDIS ABABA

Overview

After a wonderful morning in Entoto, make your way down to Shiro Meda, the city’s largest traditional clothing market and the finest spot to shop hand-woven cotton clothing, primarily for women. Following that, pay a visit to the National Museum, where Ethiopia’s oldest resident, Lucy, is housed. The next destination is the Selassie (Trinity) church, which is near to the National Museum and is one of Addis Ababa’s most prominent churches as well as Haile Selassie’s ultimate resting place. Then go to Unity Park, to see natural, cultural & historical aspects of Ethiopia in one place.
